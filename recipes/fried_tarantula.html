Fried Tarantula

A special taste, you are going to be shocked with the delicious taste.

Spicy tarantula for the good moments in family.

Ingredients

  • Tarantula (i guess).
  • Homemade spicy sauce.
  • Vegetables for decoration (to taste).
  • 100ml olive oil.
  • Salt and pepper(to taste).

Recipe instructions

  1. Seasoning the tarantula with the sauce and add pepper and salt.
  2. Fry the tarantula in the oil and wait 7min.
  3. Cut the vegetables.
  4. Set the tarantula and decorate with vegetables and sauce the dish.
  5. Enjoy, remember rejecting the girls while your eating (they're only interested in your tarantula).
