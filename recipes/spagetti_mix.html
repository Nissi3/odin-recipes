Spagetti Mix

The best tastes combination, your stomach will cry of hapiness.

A sweat and delicious noodles with chocolates and sausages.

Ingredients

  • Noodles.
  • 3 sticks of chocolate.
  • Sausages (to taste).
  • 10ml olive oil.
  • Salt (to taste).

Recipe instructions

  1. Simmer the noodles wiht salt.
  2. Cut the chocolates.
  3. Fry the sausages apart with the olive oil.
  4. Insert the chocolates into the noodles with the sausages.
  5. Enjoy with your girl.
