Corona Delicious

Amazing soup of bat that will let you dead!

The chinese cultural meal perfect for the people that want to die, this will comfort them.

Absolutely expensive recipe, difficult to found. Only the chosen can cook it.

Ingredients

  • 1 carrot.
  • Salt and pepper to taste.
  • Fresh parsley.
  • 50ml olive oil.
  • Water.
  • 1 bat (size to taste).

Recipe instructions

  1. Boil the bat apart in water with salt and pepper.
  2. Cut the carrot and the parsley.
  3. Put the bat in a bowl with the water, oil, carrot and parsley.
  4. (Optional) Remove the carrots and parsley.
  5. Enjoy.
